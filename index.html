A Few Words About Organic Farm

An organic farm is a type of agricultural system that emphasizes the use of natural, sustainable practices to grow crops and raise livestock. This means avoiding the use of synthetic pesticides, herbicides, and fertilizers, and instead relying on methods like:
Crop rotation: different crops in the same field each year helps to replenish the soil nutrients and prevent the build-up of pests and diseases.
Composting: Turning food scraps and other organic materials into nutrient-rich fertilizer.
Biological pest control: Using natural predators, such as ladybugs, to control pests
